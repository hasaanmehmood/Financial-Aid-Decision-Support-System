About




Financial crisis is faced by individuals of institutes all around the globe and considering the current situations of inflation and economic unrest, education is a luxury not everyone can afford. Institutes offer financial aid to deserving individuals. Our platform aids the decision-making committee to make better decisions and save the time and energy required for this process. The system ensures that the aid fund is utilized in the best way possible by aiding the decision-making committee with multiple insights.
